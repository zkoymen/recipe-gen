Sun-Kissed Harvest Bowl

schedule 25 Mins
groups 2 Servings
star 4.9 (124)

nutrition Nutrition Facts

Calories 420
Protein 18g
Carbs 52g
Fiber 12g

lightbulb Cook's Notes

"For an extra crunch, try roasting the chickpeas with smoked paprika. If you're short on time, a pre-washed kale mix works beautifully—just give it a quick massage with lemon juice to soften it up!"

Ingredients

2
  • 2 cups fresh kale, chopped
  • 1 large avocado, sliced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1/2 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1/4 cup lemon tahini dressing
  • 1 tsp chili flakes

Preparation Steps

1

Prep the Kale

Wash and dry the kale thoroughly. Place in a large bowl, drizzle with a bit of lemon juice, and massage for 2 minutes until it becomes tender and dark green.

2

Cook the Quinoa

Rinse the quinoa and cook according to package instructions. Fluff with a fork and let it cool slightly before adding to the bowl.

3

Assemble & Dress

Divide the kale into two bowls. Top with chickpeas, quinoa, and avocado slices. Drizzle generously with tahini dressing and sprinkle with chili flakes.
